How it works at the machine
  1. 01

    Scan to pay

    Customer scans the QR code on the machine display and pays with any UPI app.

  2. 02

    Place the cup

    Once payment confirms, the machine signals the customer to place their cup under the spout.

  3. 03

    Shake dispenses

    Powder and water mix and pour directly into the cup — ready to drink immediately.
