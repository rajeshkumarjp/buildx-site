Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What this covers

This Privacy Policy explains how BuildX handles information in connection with this website and the NutriVend machine pages listed on it. It applies site-wide, across every machine page at buildx.in/nutrivend/<machine ID>.

2. Information this website collects

This is an informational website. It does not host a checkout, does not process payments, and does not ask visitors to create an account. The only information this site may collect is standard web traffic data (such as pages visited) via basic hosting analytics.

3. Payments happen at the machine, not on this site

When you pay for a shake, you do so by scanning a QR code shown directly on the NutriVend machine's display, using your own UPI app. That payment is processed by the operator's payment provider, not by this website. BuildX does not receive, store, or have access to your payment details, bank information, or UPI ID.

4. Contact information you send us

If you email us (for example, at hello@buildx.in) or contact a machine operator directly, we or they will only use the details you share to respond to your message.

5. Cookies and tracking

This site does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. Basic, privacy-respecting hosting analytics may be used to understand overall page visits.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell or share visitor information with third parties. Information collected by an individual machine operator (such as your payment) is handled under that operator's own payment provider's privacy practices, not this website's.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: hello@buildx.in.
